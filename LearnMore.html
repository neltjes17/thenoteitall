The-Note-It-All was created for the sole purpose of helping University of Iowa students gain access to a library of study material which creates a more stress-free study environment. We hope to achieve this by providing a vast collection of notes, past exams and other study material specific to each course, found on the main page.

In addition, a collaborative study document can be accessed by clicking the green 'pen and paper' icon. Please use this document to collaborate with other students in creating study guides for upcoming quizzes or exams (great example can be seen below). Over time we plan to add as many classes as possible, but this is entirely dependent on the amount of notes we receive and your assistance! Please email us at thenoteitall@gmail.com with any class suggestions, questions or ideas.



We hope you love studying with The-Note-It-All as much as we do!
